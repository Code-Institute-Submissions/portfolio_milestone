About Me

Well, hello there! My name is Trevor and I am a full stack developer, with a keen eye for design and a passion for creating user friendly experiences. If you have a business or a passion project and want the world to see it, then I am the man with a plan that will get all eyes on you. Your expectations may be sky high but I have got the vision and the skills to bring your expectations into reality. Currently I am studying a Full Stack Development program with Code Institute where I continue to grow and perfect my skills.

my skills

From front end to back end and everything in between

Front-end

I work with all modern front-end technologies including HTML 5, CSS 3, and JavaScript. I am confident using front-end frameworks such as Bootstrap, which I use to bring your vision into reality.

ux / ui

My goal is to create eye catching designs that will grab your customers attention. The first step towards that is with intuative and easy to learn systems that are as natural to use as taking a breath.

back-end

I create fast and responsive databases that allow access to your data in a safe and scalable environment. Always have access to the data you need to keep you at the forefront.
